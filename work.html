Slalom
Technologies: C#, ASP.Net, SharePoint 2010/2013, PowerShell, TFS, Visual Studio, MS SQL, JQuery, JQuery UI, Azure Web Services, SMS
Description: Over the course of my four year tenure at Slalom Consulting, I got to craft a myriad of different systems while working in the internal IT department. From helping out with desktop and device support, all the way up to building a portal that empowered investors to share information and keep tabs on the valuation of the rapidly growing company, I was exposed to a diverse set of technologies.

For example, SharePoint was a focal system for our daily operations, enabling installation/farm set up to client-side beautifying, all the way down to building custom C# components for use in our deployment. Given its criticality to our day-to-day operations, I was continually challenged by the technology. Another area I was fortunate to explore was building a platform that would easily allow our general managers to contact their teams in case of an emergency. Built on the Azure web offerings with an integration to a third party SMS relay, I created a contact system that we actively used to keep in touch with and monitor responses from our consultants in the field. Beyond any specific technology, we specialized in creating solutions across all of our internal departments that exceeded the needs of their specialized demands.
