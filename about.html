VIDEO STORYTELLER for
the BOLD, BRAVE & TENDER

I believe in video storytelling for the bold, brave, and tender. For those who celebrate real intimacy, honest connection, and loving intentions.

In planning my own wedding, I was challenged to find a videographer that fit my vision. I wanted something emotive, relaxed, and musical. Something that illuminated the personality of my relationship with freshness and authenticity.

My clients care less about bells, whistles, and drones, and more about stripping it down to the endearing, human core. To the little moments in between.

Because the raw and the real is how we find strength.

And sharing our truths is medicinal.
